Four Data Miner

AI STEM Equity Project

Problem

Across NYC public high schools, access to STEM education varies due to differences in resources and course offerings, leaving some students underprepared for STEM careers.

Solution

We built a data-driven platform that identifies STEM gaps in NYC schools and connects underserved schools with funding for targeted support.

How AI is Used

AI analyzes school data, identifies underperforming areas, and generates recommendations to improve STEM access.

Impact

This helps reduce inequality in education and supports students in under-resourced schools.
