# Four Data Miner
## AI STEM Equity Project

### Problem
Across NYC public high schools, access to STEM education varies significantly due to differences in course offerings, academic resources, and institutional support. Schools with fewer STEM opportunities leave students underprepared and less likely to pursue higher education and careers in STEM fields.

### Solution
We built a data-driven platform that identifies NYC high schools with low STEM exposure, explains the underlying factors contributing to their underperformance, and connects them with funders for targeted support.

### How It Works
Our system assigns each school a STEM exposure score based on course offerings, academic performance, and resources. It maps underserved schools and suggests improvements.

### How AI is Used
AI analyzes school data, detects patterns, and generates insights to identify underperforming schools and recommend solutions.

### Public Interest Technology
We focused on fairness, accessibility, and equity by helping underserved schools and using data responsibly.

### Challenges
We faced challenges in finding consistent data and simplifying the idea. We solved this by focusing on key indicators.

### What We Learned
We learned how AI can support real-world decision-making and improve education outcomes.

### Next Steps
We plan to expand this into a real platform using live data and improve accuracy.
